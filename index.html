José Mourinho Analysis

This legendary quote then set the platform for José Mourinho to explode. What followed was six trophies including two Premier League trophies, two League Cups, one FA Cup and one FA Community Shield. His success followed him when he went to Inter Milan, where he won the Italian Treble with them and then with Real Madrid, where he broke countless records but more importantly, broke Barcelona’s dominance in Spanish football.
